The Hidden Gatekeeper

Why Designed Resumes Never Reach the Recruiter

Modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) don't read resumes like humans do. They convert documents into a single column of raw text. When templates use complex sidebars or graphical meters, the text is extracted in a jumbled reading order.

  • Column Mismatch Parsing

    When text reads left-to-right across two separate columns, dates get merged with wrong jobs, breaking chronological matching.

  • Skill Charts are Invisible

    Graphic meters, progress bars, and icon indicators are ignored by parser algorithms. Your 5-star rating registers as 0 keywords.

  • Unparseable Font Encodings

    Exporting from template builders often encodes letters incorrectly, turning your text into gibberish in the recruiter's search index.
